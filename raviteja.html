The Angular Forms project is another starter assignment that can help us get acquainted with the inner workings of the architecture. It demonstrates the approaches of template-driven (with NgModel) and reactive forms and how they can be tied with a backend service. Additionally, it discusses various examples of custom validators, binding to different form controls, and accessing submitted data. This is one of the most straightforward Angular projects to work on.
